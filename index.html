About Me

Classically trained violinist with over 40 years of performance experience across Europe and North America. I specialize in weddings, corporate events, and worship services, blending technical precision with spiritual sensitivity to create unforgettable musical moments.

My Musical Journey

Early Beginnings

Began playing at age 7 in Rotterdam. Joined church worship teams and the Rotterdam Youth Symphony Orchestra by age 12.

Professional Development

Performed with the Christian Radio and TV Orchestra (Evangelische Omroep) and studied at Continental Bible College in Brussels.

"I read music but can improvise too, playing from the heart and flowing in the Spirit."
