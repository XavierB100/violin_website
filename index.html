About Me

Classically trained violinist with over 40 years of performance experience across Europe and North America. I specialize in weddings, funerals, corporate events, and worship services, blending technical precision with emotional depth to create meaningful musical moments for every occasion.

My Musical Journey

Early Beginnings

Began playing at age 7 in Rotterdam. Joined church worship teams and the Rotterdam Youth Symphony Orchestra by age 12.

International Performances

Played with bands across Belgium and France, recording albums and ministering through music. Performed with the Christian Radio and TV Orchestra (Evangelische Omroep).

Current Work

Member of Brent Symphony Orchestra, session violinist, and active worship musician at HTB London.
